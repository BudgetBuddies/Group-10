2nd Live Stream Preparation

Preparation and testing of stream materials

The third quarter is over. As a new quarter comes in, new budget buddies also come in. An upgraded version of Budget Buddies was created using all the analytics obtained from the previous stream, notably from the preceding quarter. The team openly acknowledged all of the criticism and made changes in accordance with the feedback from the audience. In light of this, the following improvements have been made in order to better prepare for and present the second upcoming stream:

Layout

Promotional Materials

The team produced promotional materials to inform and advertise our upcoming live stream. In order to enhance the overall enthusiasm and depth of our brand promotion, we developed a new and enhanced poster and promotional film that include our streaming date, time, and content. Although they have similar colors and such, these advertising materials differ from those from the previous quarter since we went from a design with wavy parts to one that uses geometric shapes. Additionally, the team created raffles to hype up the stream and gain a larger audience. For a better chance of reaching more people, the streamers also shared these materials individually. You may view all of the Budget Buddies promotional materials in this Google Drive Folder.

Streamlabs Testing

Again, we thoroughly tested every component prior to the stream to avoid any additional technical issues with our streaming software. The alerts, cameras, and background music were tested once we integrated or linked our stream layout into the software.

Dry Run

In addition to setting up and testing the stream components, the streamers also practiced a dry run to simulate the upcoming live stream. Again, this assisted us in familiarizing ourselves with our outline and the manner in which we will conduct it in the actual stream. The crew was able to develop backup plans for potential technological issues and complete everything before the stream by repeatedly practicing.
